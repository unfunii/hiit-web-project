    • Bodyweight Squats

    Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Bend your knees and lower your body like sitting in a chair, then stand back up.

    Walking Lunges

    Take a big step forward, lower your body until both knees are bent, then step back to standing. Repeat with the other leg.

    High Knees

    Jog in place, lifting your knees up high with each step.

    Jump Squats

    Squat down, then jump up as high as you can. Land softly and repeat

    Calf Raises

    Rise up onto your tiptoes, then lower back down.

    Skaters

    Jump sideways to one side, landing on one foot and swinging the other behind you. Repeat, alternating sides.

    • Push-Ups

    Start in a plank position with hands shoulder-width apart, lower your body until chest nearly touches the floor, then push back up.

    Wide Arm Push-Ups

    Similar to regular push-ups but with hands placed wider than shoulder-width apart, targeting the outer chest muscles.

    Diamond Push-Ups

    Place hands close together under your chest in a diamond shape, lower your body until chest nearly touches your hands, then push back up.

    Wall Push-Ups

    Stand facing a wall, place your hands on the wall at shoulder height, then lean forward and push away from the wall.
